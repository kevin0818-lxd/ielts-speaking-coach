# IELTS Part 2 Cue Cards

Standard cue card templates for Practice Session mode. Use these when the user requests Part 2 practice. Present one cue card at a time; the user has 1 minute to prepare and 1-2 minutes to speak.

**2025 May–August 题库**: See [cue-cards-2025-may-aug.md](cue-cards-2025-may-aug.md) for 15 official cue cards from the current question bank.

---

## Describe a person

**Describe someone who has had a significant influence on your life.**

You should say:
- who the person is
- how you know them
- what they have done that influenced you
- and explain why this influence was important to you

---

## Describe a place

**Describe a place you have visited that you would like to go back to.**

You should say:
- where it is
- when you went there
- what you did there
- and explain why you would like to return

---

## Describe an experience

**Describe a memorable trip or journey you have taken.**

You should say:
- where you went
- how you travelled
- what you did during the trip
- and explain why it was memorable

---

## Describe an object

**Describe something you own that is important to you.**

You should say:
- what it is
- how long you have had it
- how you got it
- and explain why it is important to you

---

## Describe an event

**Describe a celebration or festival you have attended.**

You should say:
- what the celebration was
- when and where it took place
- what you did
- and explain why you enjoyed it or found it memorable

---

## Describe a habit or routine

**Describe something you do to stay healthy.**

You should say:
- what it is
- how often you do it
- when you started
- and explain why it helps you stay healthy

---

## Describe a skill

**Describe a skill you have learned that you are proud of.**

You should say:
- what the skill is
- how you learned it
- how long it took
- and explain why you are proud of it

---

## Describe a decision

**Describe an important decision you have made.**

You should say:
- what the decision was
- when you made it
- what the outcome was
- and explain why it was important

---

## Describe a book or film

**Describe a book or film that had a strong impact on you.**

You should say:
- what it is
- when you read or watched it
- what it is about
- and explain why it had an impact on you

---

## Describe a change

**Describe a positive change in your life.**

You should say:
- what the change was
- when it happened
- how it affected you
- and explain why it was positive
